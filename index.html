Samsung Remote


A minimalist Samsung Smart TV remote on the web. Supports 2016 and newer Samsung Smart TVs.

Instructions
  1. Find your TV's IP address on your local network; try your router admin interface or the TV's network settings.
  2. Enter the IP address in the box above and click Get Remote.
  3. Done! Remember your TV must be turned on and connected to your network for this to work.
Advanced User
  1. Prevent the TV's IP from changing. Add a DHCP reservation for your TV through your router's interface.
  2. Save the remote to your phone's homescreen. On iOS open in Safari and click Share Icon -> Add to Home Screen. On Android open in Chrome and click Menu Icon -> Add to homescreen.
Caveats
  • Requires your TV to be connected to your home network.
  • You can't turn your TV on using this remote. This is a limitation at the TV's network hardware level.
